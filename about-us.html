Our Story

Dizpose is a registered company in Nigeria established in 2010 to provide solutions to the waste management challenges facing Nigerians.
From a fleet of 5 modern waste disposal trucks and 20 staffs in 2010, dizpose now has 40 modern waste disposal trucks and ejector trailers and 200 staffs across Nigeria to collaborate with government across all levels in providing prompt and efficient waste management services.
